One Piece

Wealth. Fame. Power. The man who had everything in this world... The Pirate King, Gold Roger. The great treasure he left behind, One Piece has opened the curtain on a grand era! It is a time when eager pirates set sail, battle, and become great! The Great Age of Pirates! Words he spoke drove countless men out to sea. And so men set sights on the Grand Line, in pursuit of their dreams. The world has truly entered a Great Pirate Era!

Straw Hat Luffy

Monkey D. Luffy, the spirited protagonist of One Piece, is a young pirate with a dream to become the Pirate King. Known for his straw hat and rubber powers from the Gomu Gomu no Mi Devil Fruit, he leads the Straw Hat Pirates through epic adventures, driven by his love for freedom, loyalty, and adventure.

Characters Groups

In One Piece, characters are grouped into various key factions, each with unique goals, roles, and ideologies:

  • The Straw Hat Pirates
  • The Marines
  • The World Government and the Celestial Dragons
